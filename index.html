About Us

True North Receivables Group is a debt buying company committed to ethical portfolio acquisition and oversight. We partner exclusively with compliant, reputable third-party servicers and ensure that every account is handled in accordance with the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) and applicable state laws.

Our Values

  • Compliance-Driven: We follow FDCPA, FCRA, and all relevant state laws.
  • Vendor-Centric: We engage only with licensed and fully compliant servicers.
  • Consumer-Respectful: All accounts are handled with integrity and fairness.
  • Transparent: Our operations are built on openness and accountability.

What We Do

We purchase charged-off consumer receivables and oversee compliant recovery via vetted third-party collection agencies. Our goal is to protect consumers and creditors through clear processes, documentation, and vendor management.
