About Us

True North Receivables Group is a debt buying company committed to ethical portfolio acquisition and oversight. We do not collect directly. Instead, we partner exclusively with licensed, reputable third-party servicers who operate in full compliance with the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) and relevant state laws.

Our Values

  • Compliance-Driven: FDCPA, FCRA, and all applicable regulations guide every decision.
  • Vendor-Centric: Only proven, fully licensed collection partners are selected.
  • Consumer-Respectful: Accounts are managed with fairness, transparency, and dignity.
  • Transparent: We maintain full documentation, performance logs, and audit trails.

What We Do

We purchase portfolios of charged-off consumer receivables and ensure compliant recovery exclusively through third-party agencies. Our internal procedures include compliance monitoring, vendor oversight, and full documentation of consumer complaints, disputes, and resolution logs.
